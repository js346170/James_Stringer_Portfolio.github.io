Dipont Education: English Language Subject Panel and conference presenter

Dipont Education encompasses numerous international schools and programs across China, and I had the privilege of being a part of their English Language Subject Panel. This role involved collaborating with educators from various schools to develop and refine the English curriculum, ensuring it met the diverse needs of students. In addition to my work on the panel, I was also a speaker at the Dipont Education Conference in 2018 and 2019. My presentations focused on innovative teaching strategies and the integration of technology in the classroom, sharing insights and best practices with fellow educators.

  • English Curiculum horizontal integration
  • Developing and sharing resources accross school centers
  • Presenting MAed findings. a mixed methods study of student preperation for TOEFL
  • Student Centered learning activities sharing and discussion
  • Englsih subject panel findings for improved practice and curiculum streamlining

This experience not only enhanced my understanding of curriculum development but also allowed me to contribute to the professional growth of educators across the Dipont network. It was a rewarding opportunity to share my expertise and learn from others in the field, ultimately benefiting the students we serve.
