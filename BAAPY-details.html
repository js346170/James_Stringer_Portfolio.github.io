BA in Cultural Anthropology

I started my academic journey with a Bachelor of Arts in Cultural Anthropology from the University at Buffalo. This program provided me with a solid foundation in understanding human behavior, culture, and social structures. Studies included:

  • Research design and methodology
  • Qualitative and quantitative data analysis
  • Fieldwork techniques and participant observation
  • Cross-cultural communication and understanding
  • Ethical considerations in research
  • Data visualization and presentation skills
  • Statistical analysis using software like SAS and SPSS

This degree equipped me with the skills to conduct research, analyze data, and understand cultural dynamics. It also sparked my interest in using technology to enhance education and improve learning outcomes.
