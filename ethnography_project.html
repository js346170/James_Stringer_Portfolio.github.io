Ethnographic Research Initiative

Cultural immersion and research with ethnic minority communities in China

Project Background

I was specifically hired by Tsinghua International School for my background in Anthropology and Ethnographic research to lead the 9th-grade semester-long Ethnography Project. This initiative partners with Prop Roots to engage students in ethnographic fieldwork and social work with ethnic minority groups in China's Yunnan province near the Myanmar border.

Year 2: Yunnan Fieldwork

In the second year, we were able to travel to Yunnan for the originally planned ethnographic experience with Prop Roots.

  • Cultural Immersion: Students lived and worked with ethnic minority communities near the Myanmar border
  • Participant Observation: Engaged in daily activities, ceremonies, and community events
  • Social Work Integration: Combined research with meaningful community service projects
  • Collaborative Learning: Worked alongside Chinese department colleagues for cross-cultural understanding
  • Research Reports: Students completed comprehensive ethnographic reports based on their fieldwork
