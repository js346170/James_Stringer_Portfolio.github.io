AP Expertise

As an educator I have become something of a specialist in the College Board Advanced Placement program. I have experience with Cambridge curriculum and exams and an understanding of IB, but I have ended up mostly teaching AP. I have taught AP US history, modern world history, human geography, language and composition, literature and composition, and I have started and developed the AP Capstone Program in two different schools. As of Fall 2025 I will add two new courses, Microeconomics and Comparative Government and Politics. I have also been a reader for the AP US History exam, which means I have graded the essays of students from around the world. I have even had the head of the International AP program observe my teaching and give me feedback

  • Managed AP Capstone program and developed syllabi
