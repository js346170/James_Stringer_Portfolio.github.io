TOEFL Integration Project

Transforming English language preparation through curriculum integration

Based on recommendations from my research report at Tianjin Foreign Language School on student study habits, I led the integration of TOEFL preparation directly into the core curriculum. This innovative approach transformed how students prepared for the TOEFL exam while enhancing their overall English proficiency.

Implementation Strategy

Curriculum Integration

Embedded TOEFL vocabulary and tested skills directly into the English and Humanities program, ensuring daily exposure to exam-relevant content.

Counselor Collaboration

Partnered with College Counselors to reinforce TOEFL skills during their classes, creating a unified approach across disciplines.

Student Engagement

Achieved student buy-in through workshops demonstrating how integrated preparation reduced the need for external study centers.

Mindset Shift

Discouraged "testwise" strategies in favor of genuine language proficiency development through authentic learning experiences.

Project Outcomes

Score Improvement

Resulted in an 8% average increase in TOEFL scores among senior students within the first year of implementation.

Student Confidence

Students reported significantly greater confidence in their TOEFL preparation and English language abilities.

Satisfaction Increase

Greater student satisfaction with the school curriculum as it became more relevant to their academic goals.

Reduced Dependence

Decreased emphasis on outside study centers as students found the integrated preparation more effective.

Long-Term Impact

This approach transformed TOEFL preparation from a separate test-focused activity into an integrated component of language development. Students developed authentic language skills that not only improved their exam performance but also prepared them for academic success in English-medium universities. The project demonstrated that strategic curriculum integration can achieve better results than traditional test preparation methods while enhancing overall educational quality.
