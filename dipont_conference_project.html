Dipont Education English Subject Panel

Curriculum development and teacher support across multiple international schools

Panel Overview

As a member of the Dipont Education English Subject Panel, I participated in a collaborative group that worked to enhance English education across Dipont's network of international programs in China.

Panel Meetings

Met quarterly throughout the academic year to:

  • Streamline English subjects across programs
  • Develop standardized curriculum frameworks
  • Create shared teaching resources
  • Address common pedagogical challenges

Annual Conference

The panel's work culminated in presentations at the annual Dipont conference in August, attended by:

  • 500+ educators
  • Representatives from dozens of centers
  • Multiple stand-alone international schools

Impact & Outcomes

The English Subject Panel's work had significant impact across Dipont's educational network:

  • Created standardized curriculum frameworks used by multiple schools
  • Developed shared resource libraries for English teachers
  • Established best practices for English instruction in Chinese international schools
  • Provided professional development for hundreds of teachers
  • Fostered collaboration across different educational centers
  • Improved consistency in English education quality
