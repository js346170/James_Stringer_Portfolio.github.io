Student Magazine Initiative

Developing student journalism programs across international schools

Tianjin Foreign Language School Magazine

I founded the student magazine program as part of a journalism English class, creating a platform for students to develop their writing, editing, and publishing skills.

Print Publications (2021-22)

  • Published two print issues under my leadership
  • Featured school news, opinion pieces, student artwork, and cartoons
  • Created a collaborative space for diverse student voices
  • Developed student editorial leadership structure

The magazine became a vital part of school culture, providing students with authentic publishing experience and developing their journalistic skills.

Delta Global School Pioneer Magazine

In 2024, I brought my expertise to Delta Global School as faculty advisor for the Pioneer Media Club's student magazine.

Transformation & Direction

  • Shifted focus to current events and contemporary issues
  • Enhanced journalistic standards and research practices
  • Introduced digital publishing alongside print editions
  • Mentored student editors in leadership and decision-making
Visit Pioneer Media Club Facebook Page

Under my guidance, the magazine evolved into a more professional publication while maintaining its student-led character.

Educational Impact

The student magazine programs have provided valuable learning experiences:

Writing Development

Students developed different writing styles including news reporting, feature writing, and opinion pieces.

Critical Thinking

Researching and writing about current events enhanced critical analysis skills.

Leadership Skills

Editorial teams learned project management, collaboration, and decision-making.

Media Literacy

Students gained understanding of journalistic ethics, fact-checking, and responsible reporting.

Skills Demonstrated

  • Curriculum Integration: Embedded journalism into English curriculum
  • Publication Management: Oversaw entire publication process from concept to distribution
  • Student Mentorship: Guided students in developing their voices and skills
  • Program Development: Created sustainable magazine programs at two institutions
  • Digital Literacy: Incorporated social media and digital publishing platforms

Legacy

These student magazine programs have created lasting platforms for student expression and journalism education. At both schools, the publications continue to thrive, providing students with authentic writing experiences and developing the next generation of thoughtful communicators and critical thinkers.
